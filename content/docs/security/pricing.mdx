Subscription plans and pricing are managed in your Cochl Security billing dashboard. Hardware options and requirements are described below.

### Plans & subscription

View current plans and pricing, and manage your subscription, in the billing dashboard.

[Go to billing](https://security.cochl.ai/billing/information)

## Cochl-certified hardware

Cochl Security requires Cochl-certified hardware for AI inference. Hardware is provided by Cochl upon subscription. Hardware types differ in specs, which directly determines the number of channels that can be operated simultaneously.

Entry and Standard plans require Cochl-certified hardware. Hardware is shipped after subscription confirmation and invoiced separately.

*   Type A — up to 5 channels (Entry tier)
*   Type B — up to 20 channels (Standard tier)
*   For higher channel capacity (Enterprise tier), reach out via the [contact form](https://www.cochl.ai/contact-us/).

Hardware is invoiced separately as a one-time purchase. Your license period begins from the date you receive the hardware — not the date of payment.
