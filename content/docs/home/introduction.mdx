## What is Cochl.Sense?

**Cochl.Sense** is a machine-listening platform. It turns audio into structured insight — the sounds present, the speech being spoken, the scene as a whole — and runs anywhere your audio does, on cloud servers or a wide range of edge devices, including microcontrollers.

## What can Cochl.Sense do?

Cochl.Sense exposes three audio-understanding capabilities:

*   **Sound Event Detection** — recognize what sounds are present (sirens, glass break, baby cry, and 100+ tags). Available via the [Cloud API](/docs/cloud-api/sound-event-detection) and the [Edge SDK](/docs/edge-sdk/sound-event-detection).
*   **Speech Analysis** — transcribe speech and identify registered speakers. Available on the [Cloud API](/docs/cloud-api/speech-analysis) only.
*   **Audio Insights** — high-level summary of an audio scene (environment, situation, keywords). Available on the [Cloud API](/docs/cloud-api/audio-insights) only.
*   **Custom Sound** — extend the catalog: [Sound Events](/docs/edge-sdk/sound-event-detection/custom-sound) on the Edge SDK, or [Speaker Profiles](/docs/cloud-api/speech-analysis/custom-speaker-profile) for Speech Analysis on the Cloud API.

## How do I use Cochl.Sense?

You can use Cochl.Sense in two ways: Cochl.Sense Cloud API and Cochl.Sense Edge SDK.

#### Cochl.Sense Cloud API

Send audio (WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG) to detect sounds over HTTP.

[![Cochl.Sense Edge SDK](/images/docs/sdk1.png)](/docs/edge-sdk/getting-started)

#### Cochl.Sense Edge SDK

Run it on edge devices using Python, C++, or Android.
